Overview

Women In Tech is a unique educational program for women in Iraq, dedicated to empowering women in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

Our Mission

We aim to empower women in technology by fostering skills for future STEM leaders

Our Vision

We aim to close the gender gap in technology by empowering women with equal educational opportunities

Our Purpose

To foster the development of girls and women in STEM by connecting them with experienced mentors and motivated mentees. By creating successful matches, the program will enable future generations of girls and women to enter the field with the promise of becoming change-makers in the industry.
